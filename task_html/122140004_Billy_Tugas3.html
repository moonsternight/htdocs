About Us

El’s Coffee presents a wide selection of premium local coffee. Offering homegrown, well-harvested, carefully sorted beans, we pride ourselves in our meticulous roasting process to ensure you get the best Indonesian coffee. El’s Coffee had begun its journey in 2012, but the backstory was long before that. Our Coffee Man, Mr. Elkana Arlen Riswan was born and raised in the line of coffee families, so he was introduced to the coffee business world immediately. Ever since, he committed to growing the family business and taking it seriously until now it has become one of the most significant businesses in Lampung.

Our Coffee

El's Coffee Arabica

Kopi Arabika termasuk salah satu kopi yang terbaik di dunia. Kamu bisa mencoba kopi arabika terbaik pilihan El’s Coffee. Tersedia beragam pilihan yang dapat kamu nikmati. Ada kopi Aceh Gayo, kopi Mandheling, kopi Toraja, kopi Wamena, dan kopi Kintamani.

El's Coffee Robusta

Kopi Robusta adalah salah satu kopi yang terbaik di dunia. Kamu bisa mencoba kopi robusta terbaik pilihan El’s Coffee. Tersedia beragam pilihan yang dapat kamu nikmati. Ada kopi Flores, kopi Lanang, kopi Lampung, kopi Pagar Alam, dan kopi Java Mocha.

El's Drip Coffee

Drip Coffee atau kopi tetes El's Coffee adalah kopi hitam yang terbaik di dunia. Kamu bisa mencoba kopi drip arabika dan robusta terbaik pilihan El’s Coffee. Tersedia beragam pilihan arabika dan robusta yang dapat kamu nikmati.
